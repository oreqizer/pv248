# Statistics

The last set of exercises is focused on simple statistical
processing of data. You can either use ‹numpy› (which is quite
low-level) or ‹pandas› (which is usually much more comfortable and
high-level).

 1. ‹stats.py› – warm up with basic stats
 2. ‹frames.py› – slicing and dicing data frames [submit for .5pt]
 3. ‹student.py› – the infamous T-test
 4. ‹anova.py› – (tbd) analysis of variance
 5. ‹outliers.py› – dealing with irregularities
 6. ‹regress.py› – linear regression with outliers [submit for .5pt]
